As you can see below, you have to tune down half a step (Eb,Ab,Db,Gb) to get it
to sound just right, but you can play it with standard tuning (E,A,D,G) as long
as the rest of the band is in the right key!
This is the way I play it, it's not necesarily the way Stevie plays it live.
Stevie has a keyboard player doing the melody, so if your in a band with no
keyboard player the bassist and the guitarist should play it.
[Intro]
Gb|--------------------------------------------|
Db|----------5---------------------5-----------|  x4
Ab|----7-5-7-----5--- 5-7------5-7-----5---5-7-|
Eb|-0----------7----7------5-7-------7---7-----|

[Verse part 1]
Gb|----------------------------|
Db|----------------------------|  x4
Ab|----------------------------|
